Purpose

The purpose of the Campus Tavern is to provide the students of byui with an enjoyable alcohol free tavern asthetic where people can come to enjoy some good time to relax and socialize. It will include a soda pop bar with frier foods and pizza and there will be different activites hosted like kareoke night, comedy nights, and musical performance for all to enjoy.
